Biography and Current Research

I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from Alexandria University, Egypt. I received M.Sc. degree in wireless technologies from the Wireless Intelligent Networks Center (WINC), Nile University, Egypt. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona, in 2022.

My current research interests are:

  • Privacy Preserving Technologies

  • Wireless Communications

  • Distributed Computing

  • Machine Learning

  • Information Theoretic Security

  • Graph Analytics
