Biography and Current Research

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Princeton University, hosted by Andrea Goldsmith and Vincent Poor.

I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona, in 2022 under the supervision of Ravi Tandon and Ming Li.

My current research interests are:

  • Privacy Preserving Technologies

  • Wireless Communications

  • Distributed Computing and Sensing

  • Machine Learning Systems

  • Information Theoretic Security

  • Graph Analytics
